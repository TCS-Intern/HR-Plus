# Machine Learning Engineer

**Telentic** | Seattle, WA (Remote)

---

## About Telentic

Telentic is on a mission to make hiring fair, efficient, and intelligent. Our AI-powered platform handles everything from crafting job descriptions to generating offers. We're using cutting-edge ML to solve one of the hardest problems in business: finding the right people.

---

## About the Role

We're seeking a Machine Learning Engineer to build the AI systems at the core of our platform. You'll work on challenging problems in NLP, computer vision, and recommendation systems. This is a unique opportunity to apply state-of-the-art ML to a domain with massive impact.

---

## What You'll Do

- Design and implement ML models for resume parsing and understanding
- Build video analysis systems to assess candidate interviews
- Develop matching algorithms to connect candidates with opportunities
- Create evaluation frameworks to measure model performance and fairness
- Deploy models to production with monitoring and observability
- Collaborate with product to translate business needs into ML solutions
- Research and prototype new ML approaches

---

## ML Challenges You'll Tackle

1. **Resume Parsing:** Extract structured information from diverse document formats
2. **Candidate Matching:** Build recommendation systems to rank candidates for jobs
3. **Video Analysis:** Assess communication skills and responses using multimodal AI
4. **Bias Detection:** Ensure our models are fair and don't perpetuate hiring bias
5. **Personalization:** Customize the experience for different industries and roles

---

## What We're Looking For

### Required

- 3+ years of ML engineering experience
- Strong Python and ML framework experience (PyTorch preferred)
- Experience with NLP models and transformers
- Experience deploying ML models to production
- MS/PhD in CS, ML, or related field

### Preferred

- Experience with LLMs and prompt engineering
- Computer vision experience
- Experience with MLOps tools (MLflow, Weights & Biases, etc.)
- Published research papers
- Experience at a top ML organization (OpenAI, Google, Meta, etc.)
