# Product Manager

**TalentAI** | New York, NY (Remote)

---

## About TalentAI

TalentAI is building the future of hiring. Our AI-powered platform automates talent acquisition from job description creation to offer generation. We're making hiring faster, fairer, and more effective for companies of all sizes.

---

## About the Role

We're looking for a Product Manager to own key product areas and drive our roadmap. You'll work at the intersection of AI, design, and engineering to build products that transform how companies hire. This is a high-ownership role where you'll shape product strategy and see your vision come to life.

---

## What You'll Do

- Define product vision and strategy for your product area
- Gather and prioritize requirements from customers, sales, and stakeholders
- Write detailed PRDs and user stories for engineering
- Partner with design to create intuitive user experiences
- Analyze product metrics and run A/B experiments
- Conduct customer interviews and synthesize feedback
- Coordinate cross-functional launches with marketing and sales
- Present to leadership and the broader company

---

## Product Areas

You'll have the opportunity to own one or more of these areas:

1. **Candidate Experience:** Application flow, assessments, communication
2. **Recruiter Tools:** Job creation, candidate management, analytics
3. **AI Features:** AI-generated content, matching, insights
4. **Enterprise:** Admin tools, integrations, security

---

## What We're Looking For

### Required

- 4+ years of product management experience
- Experience with B2B SaaS products
- Strong analytical and data-driven mindset
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Experience with agile development methodologies
- Track record of shipping products customers love

### Preferred

- Experience in HR Tech or recruiting industry
- Technical background or CS degree
- Experience with AI/ML products
- MBA or equivalent
