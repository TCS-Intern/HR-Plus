## Compensation

### Base Salary
Your annual base salary will be **$210,000.00**, paid semi-monthly in accordance with the Company's normal payroll practices.

### Signing Bonus
You will receive a one-time signing bonus of **$25,000.00**, payable within 30 days of your start date. This bonus is subject to a 12-month clawback provision if you voluntarily leave the Company.

### Annual Bonus
You will be eligible for an annual performance bonus of up to **15%** of your base salary, based on individual and company performance. Bonus payments are made in Q1 following the performance year.

### Equity
Subject to approval by the Board of Directors, you will be granted **Restricted Stock Units (RSUs)** with a value of **$150,000** at the time of grant. These RSUs will vest over 4 years with a 1-year cliff:

- 25% vests after 12 months
- Remaining 75% vests monthly over the following 36 months
